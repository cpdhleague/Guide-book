Politics are a huge part of Pauper Commander, whether it’s making deals, shifting threat assessment, convincing the table, or knowing when to stay quiet. This episode we’re diving into how table politics shapes PDH games, the plays that work, the plays that backfire, and some of the unwritten rules every group seems to have.

Do politics make the game better, or do you prefer to just let the cards do the talking? Let us know how your playgroup handles it.
